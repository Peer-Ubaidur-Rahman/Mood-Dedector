ABOUT US

Meet the Tech Titans team and learn about our mission

Tech Titans is a passionate team of innovators dedicated to making AI accessible and meaningful. We believe technology should understand and respond to human emotions, creating more empathetic digital experiences.

Our flagship project, CodeSapiens Mood Detector, harnesses the power of artificial intelligence to detect and analyze emotions in real-time using facial recognition technology—all running securely in your browser.

Our Mission

To empower individuals with instant, meaningful feedback about their emotional state using privacy-conscious technology that runs 100% locally in your browser. We combine cutting-edge AI with ethical practices to create tools that enhance self-awareness and emotional intelligence.

Our Technology

CodeSapiens Mood Detector uses advanced machine learning models powered by TensorFlow.js and face-api.js to:

  • ✨ Detect faces in real-time from webcam
  • 🧠 Recognize 7 different emotions with high accuracy
  • 🔒 Process everything locally (no data sent to servers)
  • ⚡ Provide instant feedback with confidence scores
